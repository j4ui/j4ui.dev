about

16 years old from Ontario, Canada. I build Minecraft mods, race karts, and speedcube.

I got into programming by modding Minecraft and stuck with it. Most of my projects are Fabric mods written in Java. I like digging into how the game works and building things on top of it.

Outside of coding, I race karts at Goodwood Kartways. I started with arrive-and-drive at Mosport and Goodwood, won championships at both, and now compete in club racing at Goodwood. I also speedcube, with three WCA tournaments under my belt and a personal best of 27.06 seconds.
